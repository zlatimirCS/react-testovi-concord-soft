Behind every dish is a story.
These culinary leaders are here to tell you theirs.

Gaby Dalkin

Author of What's Gaby Cooking
Episode #84

Gaby Dalkin is the California-based chef and author behind the popular blog and cookbook, What's Gaby Cooking. Her career has been focused on celebrating the fresh, diverse, and experimental nature of Southern California food trends. With 13 years of successful food blogging under her belt, she explains how she makes her recipes simple, accessible, and delicious.

Curtis Duffy

Iron Chef challenger
Episode #63

Curtis Duffy competed on Iron Chef in 2022 and has earned 5 Michelin Stars. When asked  what inspires him, he answered that it's the littlest things in life. His attention to detail shows up both in his culinary successes and the way he runs his family kitchen culture, and he's joined us to explain how.

Beau MacMillan

Iron Chef America winner
Episode #67

According to Beau MacMillan, Iron Chef America winner and guest judge on Worst Cooks in America and Chopped, each meal has the potential to deeply impact our emotions. From evoking feelings of nostalgia and comfort, to bringing people together through shared memories and traditions, Beau discusses how food plays a central role in shaping each of our stories.

Elle Simone Scott

America's Test Kitchen Food Stylist
Episode #27

Elle Simone is the first African American woman to serve as the resident food stylist and test cook on America's Test Kitchen on PBS.  Her strength and perseverance while battling ovarian cancer has allowed her to build a flourishing career amidst her chronic illness, and she discusses how her setbacks inspired her to find meaning in each day.
